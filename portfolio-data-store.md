### 4. Route-Fly - GPX Route Visualization

**What it is:** A GPX-powered route visualization tool that converts running, cycling, or hiking paths into smooth, cinematic flyover animations and downloadable videos.

**Key Features:**
- GPX file upload from any source (Strava, Garmin, etc.)
- 3D flyover animation with cinematic camera movement
- High-speed playback adjustable from 50x to 500x
- HD video recording at 60 FPS, 12 Mbps
- Multiple export formats: 9:16 (Instagram) or 16:9 (YouTube)
- Free OpenStreetMap tiles (no API keys required)
- Tile preloading to prevent black boxes during recording
- Real-time animation progress indicator

**Technologies Used:**
- Frontend: React 18, Vite, TailwindCSS, Maplibre GL (3D maps)
- Video: MediaRecorder API for built-in recording
- Backend: Node.js, Express, Multer, gpxparser
- HTTP: Axios

**What makes it special:** Turn your outdoor activities into beautiful videos. No paid APIs required - completely free and open-source.

**GitHub:** https://github.com/Shashwat-s/Route-Fly

### 6. Slasher - VR-Style Rhythm Game

**What it is:** An immersive, web-based VR-style rhythm game where you use your hands to slash incoming blocks to the beat. Powered by MediaPipe for hand tracking and Three.js for 3D visuals, it offers a unique, controller-free gaming experience directly in your browser.

**Key Features:**
- **Hand Tracking:** Real-time hand detection using your webcam (no VR headset required).
- **Rhythm Gameplay:** Slash blue and pink blocks with your corresponding hands (Left Hand = Pink Saber, Right Hand = Blue Saber).
- **Audio Reactive:** Visuals and lighting pulsate to the music.
- **Dynamic Difficulty:** The game creates more challenging patterns and speeds up as you survive longer.
- **High Score System:** Track your best runs.
- **Immersive Visuals:** Neon aesthetic with post-processing effects.

**Technologies Used:**
- Frontend: React, Vite, TailwindCSS
- 3D Graphics: Three.js, React Three Fiber
- Physics: Cannon.js
- Hand Tracking: MediaPipe Hands
- State Management: Zustand
- Audio: Tone.js

**GitHub:** https://github.com/Shashwat-s/slasher
